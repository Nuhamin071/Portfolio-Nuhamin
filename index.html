                                            AddisAbaba , Ethiopia

Nuhamin Dawit is visual, Digital artist from AddisAbaba Ethiopia.
Her arts mostly focus on cultural arts but also combines
Both modern and anicent of aesthetics of Ethiopia
Her works are mostly portrait inspired from history and Tradition.
She started to sketch at young age but joined the digital art world
three years ago using her phone.
She was one of 22 female African and african-American nft artist that show case at
African nft club in chicago.
And also has shown her works on Boredcellphone art exhibition at AddisAbaba.
She still experiment from simple character to environmental design holding that deep culture.
